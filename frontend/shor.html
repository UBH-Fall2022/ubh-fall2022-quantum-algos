Shor's Algorithm

Shor's Algorithm is a quantum algorithm that can factor large numbers in polynomial time. It is a quantum version of the classical factoring algorithm known as the number field sieve. The algorithm was first published in 1994 by Peter Shor. The algorithm is based on the quantum Fourier transform and the quantum phase estimation algorithm.
